About Us

We are a group petitioning to have Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry add a new house. The school needs a new house due to too many students being sorted into houses that they do not belong to. By adding another house with different values of the current houses, new students that did not previously align with the old houses have a new option that they might align better with. We propose the new house be named Beartooth with a bear emblem. Beartooth's colors would be orange and gray. The qualities of Beartooth are patience, resilience, compassion, and communial.
